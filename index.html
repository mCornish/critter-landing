What is Critical Role?

Critical Role is a show streamed online by the Geek and Sundry network. It is, on the surface, a Dungeons and Dragons campaign run by voice actor Matt Mercer. On a deeper level, however, it takes on many roles for many different people.

For the voice actors that star in Critical Role, the show is a time to make memories with their friends. For fans, it is everything from quality entertainment to a creative outlet. It's a show by geeks, for geeks.
